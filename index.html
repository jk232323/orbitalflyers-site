Creative Commissions

Something a bit different in mind?

If you want aerial footage that no ground-based photographer can give you, get in touch. The work doesn't need to be epic or historical — sometimes the best aerial footage is a dog bolting across a field, a family farm seen from 200ft, a garden in summer from above.

The perspective changes what you see. That's the point.

Each job is a proper conversation first — where, what, why, how you want it to feel. No package deals, no fixed price lists. Just straightforward work at a fair rate.

About

John Kirkland

Filmmaker, drone pilot, and accidental historian. Based in Ayrshire, Scotland.

OrbitalFlyers started as an aerial photography business and became something more interesting: a documentary channel that explores Scottish history from the air, on foot, and in the archives. The drone is just the tool. The history is the point.

CAA-certified, A2 competency, operating across Scotland with permissions at Historic Environment Scotland-managed sites including Edinburgh Castle, Linlithgow Palace, and Rosslyn Chapel.

Flying a DJI Mini 5 Pro and DJI Mavic 4 Pro. Available for commissions across Scotland and beyond.
